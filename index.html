The Process

  • 1

    Gather initial requirements and estimates

    We learn more about your goals, what product you want to build, your expected outcome, and the type of engagement you require.

  • 2

    Develop project plan and assemble team

    We create a plan for your product - features, timelines, architecture. We work with you and select the ideal team.

  • 3

    Execute: Design, Coding, Testing

    Once the plan is finalized, our team gets to work on bringing the project to reality -- designing, coding, testing features at regular intervals. Meanwhile, we continue to provide you with frequent updates (and demos).

  • 4

    Deploy and maintain

    Once the project is fully developed and tested to your satisfaction, we deploy and integrate it to your requirements. We also provide an option to help you maintain the product after it is deployed.
